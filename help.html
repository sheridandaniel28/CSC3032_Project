Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my child's profile?

You can update your child's profile by going to Settings > Account. There you can change their name, age, and learning preferences.

Why aren't the sounds working?

First, check your device's volume settings. Then go to Settings > Sound Settings to make sure sound effects are turned on. If it still doesn't work, try refreshing the page.

How do I reset my progress?

To reset progress, go to Settings and scroll to the bottom. You'll find a "Reset Progress" button. Note: This cannot be undone!

Can I use this on multiple devices?

Yes! Just log in with the same account on any device to access your child's profile and continue their learning journey.

How do I report a problem?

You can email us at help@tinylearners.com or use the "Contact Us" form below. We'd love to hear from you!
